Sync O'clock Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 25, 2025

Introduction

Sync O'clock is a clock application that shows you the time in a location where it's 5 o'clock. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data and your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

Sync O'clock is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • Time zone data: We use your device's time zone to calculate and display times in other locations.
  • Widget preferences: We store your widget display preferences (analog/digital, 12/24 hour format) locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to:

  • Display accurate times in different time zones
  • Remember your widget display preferences
  • Provide a personalized experience based on your settings

Data Storage

All your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store any personal data on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Sync O'clock does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data stored by the app (all stored locally on your device)
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • Modify your preferences at any time through the app's settings

Children's Privacy

Sync O'clock does not collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
